AC & Heating Repair

Keep your cabin comfortable with expert AC and heating diagnostics.

Schedule this service

Overview

A malfunctioning AC or heater can make driving miserable. We diagnose and repair all climate control systems, from refrigerant leaks to blower motor failures. Enjoy comfortable driving year-round.

What's included

  • Quick cooling and heating
  • Eliminates unpleasant odors
  • Prevents system damage
  • Improves defogging performance

How it works

1

System Check

We test pressure, temperature, and airflow to identify the problem.

2

Leak Detection & Repair

Using UV dye and electronic detectors, we find and fix leaks, then recharge the system.

3

Performance Verification

We run the system to ensure proper operation and comfortable cabin temperatures.

Common questions

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

Common causes include low refrigerant, a faulty compressor, or a clogged condenser. We'll diagnose the exact issue.

How often should I service my car's AC?

We recommend an AC check every two years or if you notice reduced cooling performance, strange noises, or odors.
