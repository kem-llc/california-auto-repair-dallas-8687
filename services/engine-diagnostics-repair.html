Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Expert diagnosis and repair for check engine lights, performance issues, and more.

Overview

Our advanced diagnostic equipment quickly identifies the root cause of engine problems — from misfires to sensor failures. We then provide a clear, written estimate before any repair work begins. Whether it's a simple spark plug replacement or a complex timing belt job, we restore your engine's performance and reliability.

Common questions

Why is my check engine light on?

It could be anything from a loose gas cap to a faulty oxygen sensor. We recommend bringing it in for a diagnostic scan to find the exact issue.

How long does an engine diagnostic take?

Most diagnostics are completed within an hour. If the issue is complex, we may need additional time for a thorough inspection.
