Common questions

How often should I replace my brake pads?

Typically every 30,000–50,000 miles, but it depends on driving habits. We inspect them at every service and let you know when it's time.

What does a brake fluid flush do?

Brake fluid absorbs moisture over time, which can cause corrosion and reduce braking performance. A flush removes old fluid and replaces it with fresh, high-boiling-point fluid.
