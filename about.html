Our story

Your Trusted Local Auto Repair Team

To provide honest, high-quality automotive repair services that keep Dallas drivers safe and satisfied.

California Auto Repair was founded with a simple mission: deliver expert auto repair with integrity. Our owner, a seasoned mechanic with over 15 years of experience, saw a need for a shop that prioritized customer education and fair pricing over profit. Starting with a small team, we built a reputation for reliability one vehicle at a time.

Today, we serve drivers across Dallas with a full range of services, from routine oil changes to complex engine overhauls. Our certified technicians stay ahead of the curve with ongoing training, and our commitment to transparency means every customer knows exactly what their car needs and why. We're proud to be a trusted name in the community.
